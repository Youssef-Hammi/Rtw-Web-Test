Ruin The Weak is a powerhouse metal outfit forged with passion in mind. Hailing from Tunisia, they deliver a relentless sonic assault that fuses crushing riffs, thunderous vocals, and soaring melodies. Formed in July 2022, Ruin The Weak faced their fair share of challenges, but their unwavering dedication to their art has led them to grow as musicians and individuals. Witnessing a Ruin The Weak performance is an electrifying experience. On stage, they channel the raw energy of music, creating an intense atmosphere that leaves audiences mesmerized and craving for more. Unapologetically heavy, their music/lyrics draws inspiration from the darker corners of the human psyche and subconscious mind. With influences ranging from metal legends to modern pioneers. they create a signature sound that transcends metal genres.
